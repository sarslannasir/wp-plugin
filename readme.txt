== Description ==

WP Product Selector makes it easy to deploy guided quiz style product selectors on your WooCommerce site so that consumers can find the perfect product for their specific requirements. Let your customers pick the right product based on their specific requirements.

**Plugin Demo**
[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OAz0Bg0otsw]

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire '/wp-product-selector' directory to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory.
2. Activate WP Product Selector through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
